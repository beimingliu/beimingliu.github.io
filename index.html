LEGOIT

Many of us face the challenge of getting a unique birthday/anniversary gift for their loved one or family members. LEGOIT.us is a web application that transforms your photos into a Lego set with Instagram-like filters and helps you turn memorable moments into priceless gifts. The algorithm will apply desired filters and match the colors between the photos and Lego bricks.

What does Trump say? (and how stock market reacts)

President Trump is probably the most active politician on Twitter. What does he say on Twitter? Do his tweets have an impact on the stock market? In this project, I scraped all his tweets since 01-01-2017, performed Natural Language Processing, visualized the S&P 500 index movement and tweets on the same plot to identify whether his tweets impacted the stock market.

Global Trade Visualization

Understanding Global Trade is hard but it doesn't have to be! The visualization project by @TimLee and me aim to utilize many data sources to provide a visualize the interwoven nature of international industries and to show the interdependence of nations.
